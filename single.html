Importance of changing Vehicle filter

Vehicle filters plays an important role to run your Car like a Champion. It can cause the problem and affect the performance if any of the filter get blocked. Sometimes you had noticed dirty smoke, loss of power, oil light or even engine failure.
In this article we’ll look into the different types of filters of car and the requirement of replacing them regularly.
Every car has 4 main filters:-
(1) Cabin Filter
(2) Air filter
(3) Oil filter
(4) Fuel filter

The function of all the filters is to enable flows and catch impurities in fuel, in motor oil and in dust.
To perform the car well and more efficient it is required to change the filter on time, if not they won’t work properly at the end also it could cause an impact on performance. Let’s have closer look:
(1) Cabin Filter: - It cleans the air that comes into the interior of your car the heating and air Conditioner. It filter out dust and other airborne particles.

  • It cleans the air of your car
  • Provide fresher air to breath
  • It increase the life of car’s AC and heating system

(2) Air Filter: - Just like human needs oxygen to breathe, similarly car needs oxygen for the combustion process. The air filter prevents the engine from dust, particles and ensure the good mixture of air and oil reaching to engine. Car’s air filter comes in circular shape, cylindrical shape and in planes. Air filter helps in providing

  • For better car performance: - Since the clean air flows in engine, the mixture of air and oil gives maximum power to engine.
  • To save fuel: - Air filter decrease the fuel consumption by ensuring good combustion process.

(3) Oil Filter: - Just like other filter oil filter also helps to remove dirt, oxidizes oil, metallic particle from the oil (that may appear in the motor oil due to engine wear) and ensure continuous oil flow. Reasons to change oil filter

  • To reduce engine wear: - A faulty air filet could allow impurities to pass which can cause of early engine wear. It could be the reason of poor performance and engine failure.
  • To avoid soiling your new oil: - To avoid the soiling your new oil, the oil filter need to be changed in every
    • 10,000 KM for Petrol
    • 15,000 KM for Diesel

(4) Fuel Filter: - Fuel filter helps to remove impurities from fuel. It helps to protect your fuel injector by eliminating dust, rust and grit contained in the fuel, and it plays an important role to make the better performance of car engine.
Reasons to change fuel filter

  • To maintain your engine performance
  • To keep your car engine parts in good condition
